K-Dharura

The K-Dharura platform solves some of the Kenya’s unsolved catastrophic emergencies and accidents. K-Dharura tries to raise the preparedness for disaster to a better percentage enough to save more lives.This is done by involving private institutions, NGOs and government parastatals.
