The K-Dharura system solves some of the Kenya’s unsolved catastrophic emergencies and accidents. Our platform raises the preparedness for disaster to a better percentage enough to save more lives as compared to the current situation. This is done by gradually involving private institutions, NGOs and government parastatals as a package.

The system provides communication platform between the various parties to be involved during an occurence of an emergency in order to provide a quick response to the affected and save more lives
